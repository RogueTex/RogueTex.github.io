Plant Disease Detection

Published on: September 8, 2018

This project utilises Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN) and PyTorch to classify plant diseases based on leaf images, enabling early detection and treatment recommendations.

Project Objectives

  • Accurately detect and classify plant diseases using CNN models.
  • Provide early detection and treatment recommendations.
  • Help farmers and agricultural experts minimize crop loss.

Outcomes

The model achieved high accuracy in detecting multiple plant diseases such as early blight and leaf rust, with a robust system for providing treatment suggestions based on the detected disease.
